Roofing FAQs

Everything you wanted to know about your roof.

How long does a new roof take to install?

Most residential roof replacements take 2–4 days depending on size, pitch, and material. Tile and metal roofs take a bit longer than composition shingle. Commercial flat roofs typically run 3–10 days. We give you a firm timeline in your written estimate.

How much does a new roof cost in Los Angeles?

Asphalt shingle averages $8–$15/sq ft installed, tile $12–$22/sq ft, and standing-seam metal $15–$30/sq ft in the LA market. Variables include tear-off layers, deck condition, pitch, access, and permit fees. Every estimate we give is written, itemized, and honest — no bait-and-switch.

Do you pull permits?

Yes. Every re-roof or structural repair is permitted through the appropriate LA County or city building department. Permits protect you — they ensure the work meets California Building Code and won't cause title or insurance issues later.

What warranties do you offer?

Manufacturer warranties on materials (typically 30–50 years for asphalt, 50+ for tile and metal) plus our own workmanship warranty. We walk through all warranty documents with you at project close-out.

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes — CA Contractor's State License Board License #1109337. We carry general liability and workers' comp insurance. You can verify our license any time at cslb.ca.gov.

Do you work with insurance claims?

Yes. We work with most major carriers on storm damage, hail, and wind claims. We'll document damage, provide photos and a detailed scope, and coordinate with your adjuster.

What parts of Los Angeles County do you serve?

All of it. San Fernando Valley, Santa Clarita Valley, San Gabriel Valley, Westside, South Bay, Long Beach, downtown, Antelope Valley — if it's in LA County, we service it. See our full service area list.

Do you offer free inspections?

Yes — free, no-obligation roof inspections with a written report. If you just want to know how much life your roof has left, we'll tell you honestly and won't pressure you to replace it early. Request a free inspection.

What roofing materials do you install?

Composition (asphalt) shingles, clay and concrete tile, standing-seam and panel metal, TPO and modified bitumen flat roofing, SPF spray polyurethane foam, and built-up roofing. We also install skylights and seamless aluminum gutters.

How do I know if my roof needs replacement vs. repair?

Age is the first clue — asphalt roofs past 20 years and tile underlayment past 25–30 often need full replacement. Other warning signs: widespread granule loss, repeated leaks, sagging deck, visible daylight from the attic, or storm damage over 30% of the surface. A free inspection will give you a clear answer.
