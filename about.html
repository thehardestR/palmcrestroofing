About Palm Crest Roofing

Licensed Los Angeles County Roofing Contractor

Experienced roofers serving LA County — honest pricing, quality materials, and workmanship that lasts.

Palm Crest Roofing is built on a simple idea: homeowners and business owners across Los Angeles County deserve a roofing contractor who shows up on time, explains the work clearly, and stands behind every nail. We're a fully licensed California roofing contractor (CA Lic. #1109337) focused on high-quality residential and commercial roofing across LA County.

Our crews handle everything from small repairs to complete tear-offs and re-roofs. We install composite shingle, clay and concrete tile, standing-seam metal, TPO and modified bitumen flat roofing, SPF spray foam, and seamless aluminum gutters. Every project starts with a free, no-pressure inspection and a clear written estimate.

We serve homeowners from Santa Clarita to Long Beach, from the Westside to the San Gabriel Valley — and everywhere in between. Whatever the neighborhood, the commitment is the same: quality materials, skilled installers, and a workmanship warranty you can count on.
