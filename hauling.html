Frequently Asked Questions

What areas do you serve for hauling?
We provide dump trailer rental and junk removal services throughout Los Angeles County — from the San Fernando Valley to the South Bay, Pasadena, Long Beach, and everywhere in between.
What items can't you haul?
We cannot haul hazardous materials, wet paint, chemicals, asbestos, or medical waste. If you're unsure about a specific item, please call us!
How does the "Pick Up Only" service work?
With Pick Up Only, you load the trailer yourself. We pick it up, drive it to the dump, and you pay the dump fees directly to the facility. It's our most cost-effective option for DIYers.
