Frequently Asked Questions

Do you haul roof tear-off debris and old shingles?
Yes — roof tear-off debris is one of our most common jobs. Asphalt shingles, clay tile, concrete tile, felt underlayment, nails, and flashing all accepted. Whether you're a homeowner doing a DIY roof replacement or a contractor needing the site cleared fast, we've got you covered throughout LA County.
How much does junk removal cost in Los Angeles?
Most junk removal jobs in LA run $150–$600 depending on volume, debris type, distance to the dump, and whether you need full-service loading. Use the instant quote calculator above to get your personalized price in seconds.
What areas do you serve for hauling?
We serve all of Los Angeles County — from the San Fernando Valley (Burbank, Glendale, Sherman Oaks, Van Nuys) to the South Bay (Torrance, Manhattan Beach, Redondo Beach), Pasadena, Long Beach, Santa Clarita, and everywhere in between.
What is the difference between trailer rental and full-service hauling?
Trailer rental: we drop off a dump trailer, you load it at your pace, we haul it away. Full service: our crew does all the loading for you. Pick-up only: you've already loaded it, we just tow it to the dump (most affordable option).
Do you offer same-day junk removal?
Yes, same-day service is available based on schedule. Call 818.252.9422 in the morning and we'll do our best to get there that day. We cover all of Los Angeles County.
What items can't you haul?
We cannot haul hazardous materials: wet paint, chemicals, asbestos, motor oil, propane tanks, or medical waste. Everything else — construction debris, shingles, furniture, appliances, yard waste, tires — is fine. Call us if you're unsure about a specific item.
