Commercial Roofing Across Los Angeles County

Palm Crest Roofing serves commercial property owners, managers, and tenants across LA County with full-service commercial roofing. From small retail buildings to large warehouses and multi-tenant HOAs, we handle re-roofs, recovers, repairs, and preventative maintenance on every major low-slope system in use today.

Key Benefits

  • TPO, PVC, modified bitumen, built-up, and SPF foam systems
  • Minimal business disruption — flexible scheduling including nights/weekends
  • Coordination with property managers, HOAs, and tenants
  • Comprehensive insurance and bonding
  • Detailed written scopes for budget planning and RFPs
  • Preventative maintenance contracts available

Our Process

Step 1: Site Survey

We inspect the roof, document existing conditions, identify drains and penetrations, and take core samples if needed.

Step 2: Proposal

Detailed scope and pricing — broken out by system, warranty, and options so you can compare apples-to-apples.

Step 3: Pre-Construction Meeting

Coordinate logistics with your facilities team: access, parking, noise, safety, and tenant communication.

Step 4: Installation

Licensed crew, proper fall protection, material loading plan, and daily site cleanup.

Step 5: Close-Out

Final inspection, as-built documentation, and manufacturer warranty registration.

Commercial Roofing FAQs

What commercial roofing systems do you install?

TPO, PVC, modified bitumen (torch-down and cold-applied), built-up, SPF foam, and standing-seam metal. We'll recommend the right system based on your building type, budget, and local climate.

Do you offer preventative maintenance?

Yes. We offer annual and semi-annual maintenance contracts that extend roof life and catch issues before they become leaks.

Can you work around our business hours?

Absolutely. We regularly work nights, weekends, and phased installs to minimize disruption to tenants and operations.
