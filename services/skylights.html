Skylight Installation & Replacement

Skylights bring natural light deep into rooms that windows can't reach — but they're also the #1 source of roof leaks when installed poorly. Palm Crest Roofing installs and replaces VELUX, Fakro, and custom skylights with proper curb, flashing, and underlayment integration so they don't leak for decades.

Key Benefits

  • Fixed, venting, and solar-powered venting skylights
  • VELUX, Fakro, and custom-sized units
  • Proper curb construction and step-flashing integration
  • Ice-and-water shield around every opening
  • Manufacturer warranty registration
  • Tubular skylights (solar tubes) for small spaces

Our Process

Step 1: Consultation

Walk through skylight options, sizes, operation (fixed vs venting), and placement.

Step 2: Opening & Framing

Cut and frame opening with proper headers, following structural requirements.

Step 3: Curb Construction

Build waterproof curb with correct height, slope shedding, and ice-and-water shield.

Step 4: Flashing

Manufacturer step-flashing or custom pan/counterflashing integrated into the roof underlayment.

Step 5: Interior Finish

Drywall the opening shaft, prime, and paint to match your ceiling.

Skylight Installation FAQs

Do skylights leak?

Quality skylights properly installed with correct curb, flashing, and underlayment integration do not leak. Most skylight leaks come from failed installation — poor flashing or wrong-size curb — not defective product.

How much do skylights cost installed?

Fixed skylights: $1,500–$3,500 installed. Venting skylights: $2,500–$5,000. Solar venting: $3,000–$6,000. Prices vary by size, roof type, and whether interior finish is included.

Can I add skylights during a re-roof?

Yes — that's actually the ideal time. Adding or replacing skylights is far cheaper and easier during a roof replacement since the underlayment and flashing can be integrated seamlessly.
