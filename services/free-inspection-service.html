Free Roof Inspection — LA County-Wide

A proper roof inspection tells you three things: (1) how much life your current roof has left, (2) whether any repairs are needed now, and (3) what options make sense if a replacement is coming. Palm Crest Roofing does free, no-obligation inspections across all of Los Angeles County — and if your roof is fine, we'll tell you.

Key Benefits

  • Full exterior roof walk-through (when safely accessible)
  • Attic inspection for leaks, ventilation, and insulation
  • Flashing, valley, and penetration assessment
  • Gutter and downspout check
  • Photo documentation of all findings
  • Written report with honest life-expectancy estimate

Our Process

Step 1: Schedule

Call 818.252.9422 or request online. We usually have slots within a few days.

Step 2: Exterior Inspection

Our inspector walks the roof (when safe), checks shingles/tiles, flashings, valleys, penetrations, and gutters.

Step 3: Attic Inspection

Most leaks show up in the attic before they reach your ceiling — we check for stains, moisture, proper insulation, and ventilation.

Step 4: Photo Documentation

Every finding is photographed so you can see exactly what we saw.

Step 5: Written Report

You get a plain-language report with condition, issues found, recommended actions, and pricing if work is needed.

Free Roof Inspection FAQs

Is the inspection really free?

Yes, truly free — no fine print, no hidden fees, no obligation. We earn our living by earning trust first.

Will you try to sell me a new roof?

No. If your roof has another 5–10 years, we'll tell you. We'd rather earn your trust for the replacement years from now than pressure you today.

How long does an inspection take?

Typically 30–60 minutes for residential, longer for large commercial buildings or complex roofs.
