Roof Maintenance FAQs

How often should my roof be inspected?

Annually at minimum for composition roofs; semi-annually (spring and fall) for tile, flat, and commercial roofs. More often if you have heavy tree cover.

What's included in a maintenance visit?

Inspection, debris removal, valley and gutter cleaning, flashing and sealant touch-ups, and minor repairs up to a set value. Major repairs are quoted separately.

Do maintenance programs really save money?

Yes — dramatically. Most premature roof failures are caused by small issues (clogged drains, failed sealant, missing flashing) that go unnoticed until they cause major damage. Catching them early costs a fraction of the repair bill.
