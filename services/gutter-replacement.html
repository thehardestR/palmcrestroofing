Seamless Gutter Replacement in LA County

Gutters protect your siding, windows, foundation, and landscaping from roof runoff. When they're undersized, clogged, or leaking, water finds its way where it shouldn't. Palm Crest Roofing installs seamless aluminum gutters — formed on-site to the exact length of your roof line — along with downspouts and gutter guards.

Key Benefits

  • Seamless aluminum gutters in 5" and 6" K-style
  • Half-round and custom profiles available
  • 20+ color options to match your trim or fascia
  • Properly sized downspouts and downspout extensions
  • Leaf Relief, Gutter Helmet, and micro-mesh gutter guards
  • Warrantied workmanship — no leaking seams

Our Process

Step 1: Measure & Inspect

Measure roof line, identify downspout locations, check existing fascia for rot or damage.

Step 2: Fascia Repair

If the fascia board is damaged, we replace it before hanging gutters — critical for secure mounting.

Step 3: On-Site Forming

Seamless aluminum is formed to exact length on-site from coil stock — no field seams.

Step 4: Hanging & Sloping

Gutters hung with hidden hangers at proper slope (1/4" per 10 feet) toward downspouts.

Step 5: Downspouts & Extensions

Downspouts mounted, sealed, and extended away from foundation.

Gutter Replacement FAQs

Why 'seamless' gutters?

Traditional sectional gutters have a seam every 10 feet — and seams eventually leak. Seamless gutters are formed on-site to the exact length of your roof line with only end caps, corner miters, and downspout outlets as seams.

Do I need gutter guards?

If you have trees overhanging your roof, yes — they drastically reduce maintenance and prevent clogs. We install Leaf Relief, micro-mesh, and other quality guard systems.

How much do seamless gutters cost?

Typical residential installs run $8–$14 per linear foot for 5" K-style, $10–$18/ft for 6", and $15–$25/ft for half-round copper or specialty profiles. Final pricing depends on height, access, and downspout count.
