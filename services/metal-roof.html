Metal Roofing FAQs

Is a metal roof louder in the rain?

No — properly installed metal roofs over a solid deck with underlayment are no louder than asphalt or tile. The 'tin roof in the rain' sound comes from metal over open purlins on barns, not residential installs.

How long does a metal roof last?

50+ years for standing-seam; 40+ for stone-coated steel. It is often the last roof a home will ever need.

Will metal fit a Spanish-style home?

Stone-coated steel panels can mimic tile or shake profiles and fit traditional California architecture well. We'll show you samples during your free inspection.
