Roof Repair Across LA County

Not every roof problem needs a full replacement. Palm Crest Roofing fixes leaks, damaged flashing, blown-off shingles, cracked tiles, storm damage, and pinhole leaks on flat roofs — often the same week you call. If your roof has good life left, we'll repair it honestly rather than push a replacement you don't need.

Key Benefits

  • Leak diagnosis — pinpoint the actual source, not just visible damage
  • Asphalt shingle, tile, metal, and flat-roof repair
  • Flashing repair at walls, chimneys, skylights, and valleys
  • Emergency tarp service during storms
  • Insurance claim documentation and photos
  • Honest assessments — we'll tell you if a repair doesn't make sense

Our Process

Step 1: Leak Inspection

Roof walk + attic inspection to find the actual source. Leaks often travel along rafters before dripping — the visible drip rarely shows the real problem.

Step 2: Written Quote

Clear estimate with options: targeted repair, full section replacement, or full re-roof if needed.

Step 3: Repair

Flashing, shingle, tile, membrane, or penetration repair done to code with matching materials.

Step 4: Verification

Water test on completed repair. Photo documentation of work for your records and any insurance claim.

Step 5: Follow-Up

We stand behind every repair. If it leaks again within the warranty period, we come back and fix it.

Roof Repair FAQs

Can you tarp my roof today?

During storms we prioritize emergency tarps and temporary repairs. Call 818.252.9422 and we'll get someone to you as fast as possible.

How much does a roof repair cost?

Targeted repairs (single flashing, shingle, or tile) run $300–$900. Larger section repairs run $1,000–$3,000. Flat-roof seam repairs vary by extent. Every repair gets a written quote before we start.

Will a repair void my manufacturer warranty?

Not when done properly with matching materials and to manufacturer spec. We follow manufacturer installation guidelines on every repair.
