Which Roofing Material Is Right for Your Home?

Asphalt composition shingle remains the most popular choice for LA County homes — affordable, durable, and available in a wide range of styles. Tile (clay or concrete) fits Mediterranean and Spanish-style homes beautifully and can last 50+ years. Standing-seam metal is increasingly popular for its durability, energy efficiency, and modern look. We'll walk you through the tradeoffs during your free inspection and help you choose the right system for your home and budget.

Residential Roofing FAQs

How long does a residential re-roof take?

Most homes are 2–4 days. Larger or steep-pitch homes, tile, and metal may take a day or two longer.

Do you handle permits?

Yes — we pull all required permits through your city or LA County and handle the final inspection.

Will you remove the old roof?

Yes. We always recommend a full tear-off rather than an overlay — it's the only way to catch hidden deck damage and install a roof that lasts.
